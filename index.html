The headline

~400–500 hours of skilled work, compressed into a system that runs unattended.

One complete lead — finding it, auditing it, cross-checking five data sources, writing the email, passing 18 quality checks — is roughly 3–4 hours of skilled human work by hand. The pipeline does it in minutes. Across ~313 contacted businesses and 121 full audits, that's the equivalent of about three months of full-time manual labour — built and run by one person.

The builds

Six things shipped, in order of weight

01

MaxContentSEO Pipeline the core engine

An end-to-end acquisition machine for local beauty, wellness and TCM businesses. It finds qualified leads, builds a defensible SEO audit, writes a legally compliant (UWG/DSGVO) outreach email, and drops it ready-to-send. The only manual step is the final "send" click.

1 Lead selection 2 5-source data gathering 3 Second-model cross-check 4 Draft creation 5 18-point quality gate 6 Roast pass 7 Email verification 8 Draft filing
Proof it's real, not a toy

349 commits, 228 documented pipeline runs, and 27 documented auto-aborts — the quality filter actively stops bad emails from going out rather than shipping junk.

02

gego AI-visibility tracker · Go

A self-built command-line tool in Go that tracks how a brand appears in AI answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) — the "are we cited by the AI?" question classic SEO tools can't answer. Compiles to real binaries (gego.exe, lingqi-tracker.exe), runs weekly, ships with proper repo structure.

The rarest asset here

LLM-visibility tracking barely exists as a product in the German-speaking market. Built once, it now runs on its own — the only strand of the business that scales without costing more of my time.

03

blitztext voice-to-text · Python

One of the very first tools built (20 Apr 2026). A global-hotkey speech-to-text tool — Ctrl+Shift, speak, text appears anywhere — plus a video transcription module for Skool/Vimeo/Loom (inspired by Christoph Magnussen ↗, built on my own).

The clearest time-saving example

Dictation runs ~3× faster than typing. Across ten weeks where nearly everything was written, that turns 2–3 hours of daily typing into under an hour of speaking — a cumulative saving of dozens of hours. It paid for its own build time within days.

04

GEO Product lead magnet + collateral

A complete GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) guide, versions 1 through 5, plus a landing-page brief ("AI-Visibility-Check") and supporting articles. The funnel's front door — paid entry product and lead magnet feeding the consulting business.

Packaged, not one-off

Five iterated versions represent multiple days of freelance copywriting — but more importantly, they turn internal GEO know-how into a repeatable asset rather than advice given once.

05

geo-audit Skills citability scoring

A suite of Claude skills — geo-audit, geo-compare, geo-monitor, geo-fix-content, geo-fix-schema — that score a website's citability by AI across four dimensions.

Fuzzy judgment → repeatable score

What would be half a day of manual analysis per site becomes a structured skill run — and the same skills power both the consulting service and the gego product.

06

MaxContentSEO Website live · Jekyll

A public Jekyll site on GitHub Pages — blog, industries, case studies, legal pages, audit examples. Live, and serving as the brand's front, with the content engine feeding it automatically. maxcontentseo.de ↗

An agency-scale deliverable, solo

A custom multi-section business site with a content pipeline behind it is typically a multi-week agency project costing several thousand euros. Built and maintained alone.
